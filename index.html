What We Do


Drawing from our African Leadership Toolkit (ALT), LeadAfrique International works to cultivate a cadre of leaders who are values-oriented, confident, solutions-oriented, and disciplined to do meaningful work and to transform their environments for good.

Our 8-Step Leadership Framework

AWARE

Stay informed and conscious of your surroundings.

ORGANIZE

Structure and arrange tasks for optimal efficiency.

PRESENCE

Be fully engaged and focused in the present moment.

VOICE

Express thoughts and ideas with clarity and confidence.

LEAD

Guide and direct others towards a common goal.

REFLECT

Review actions and outcomes to learn and improve.

RESPOND

Take action and react appropriately to situations.

STEP OUT

Move beyond your comfort zone and engage boldly.
