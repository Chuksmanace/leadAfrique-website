GIVE YOURSELF PERMISSION TO SUCCEED

#LeadAfriqueInternational Feb 26, 2024

It’s never too late. It’s never too early to start all over again and give yourself the permission to succeed. Giving yourself permission to succeed is a powerful act of self-belief and empowerment. https://youtu.be/nEuE-IcOW6Y?si=oodLQQZRPCZQ9peO

Success is often hindered by self-imposed limitations, other people’s expectations and definitions of success not to mention lack of belief in yourself. https://youtu.be/LZ4T16BsZKg?si=Qjy4tuaYBJIGcILg

To give yourself permission to succeed means to;

* What’s holding you back. These are mostly the stories we tell ourselves to keep us bound. https://youtu.be/7P-r7xgC5G0?si=4fh229qOp_Sf3Jxv

* Interrogate your actions and inactions. https://youtu.be/nyblC-l0e9c?si=kJYD1wU7HUYxua0R

* From whence do you take decisions? What’s the root cause of the decisions you are taking? Is it a fear of failure? A pronouncement of failure from a person you hold dear? Your belief systems?

Success comes in different shapes and sizes not reserved for a select few. Success is attainable.

Question is; will you realize success when it comes to your doorstep?
To truly succeed, you must break free from the shackles of self-doubt and step into your full potential.

Recognize that you are capable of achieving great things and are deserving of all the opportunities that come your way - take risks, pursue your passions, and overcome obstacles with resilience and determination.

Silence your inner critic and replace negative self-talk with self-affirming beliefs.

Remember, success looks different for everyone. It's not just about material wealth or external accolades; it's about living a fulfilling and purposeful life aligned with your values and aspirations. https://youtu.be/DjZ7PJD3Avo?si=j8gExrN5BcILB0cp

To give yourself permission to succeed, start by:

* Renewing your mind
* Pattern interrupt your thoughts
* Reframe your judgements
* Challenge your limiting beliefs
* Choose a path to success and take actions to pursue and achieve them.
* Be intentional about who gains admission into your inner circle of influence
* Get an accountability partner who isn’t afraid to tell you the truth.
* Learn. Unlearn. Relearn. https://youtu.be/cKMtFa0RBOI?si=BklyIGjArx_XrHg1

The only permission you need to succeed is the one you give yourself. Give yourself the gift to succeed.
